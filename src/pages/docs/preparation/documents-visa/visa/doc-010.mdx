# 签证 - 赴美实习项目签证问答汇总

## 赴美实习项目签证问答汇总

## ▼

根据美国移民法214（b）条款的解释，任何申请美国非移民签证的申请者都会被默认为是具有移民倾向。凡是不能够消除签证官的这种有罪假设的申请者，就会毫无疑问地被拒绝。

因此申请人必须在短短的两分钟的面试时间里说服签证官自己没有移民倾向。

虽然我们了解签证官可能提出的所有问题，但是需要特别强调的是，自信、微笑、与签证官有目光交流的回答方式往往比答案本身对签证官更具有影响力。

根据移民法的要求，美国的签证官会从如下两个个方面来考虑申请人的移民倾向是否成立。

## ▼

## 第一方面

## 去美国的目的

美国的移民法首先要求每一位签证申请人有合理合法的访问理由。鼓励每一位学生根据下列问题的答案熟记去美国的理由。签证官可能会问到下面的问题：

## 去美国的目的

☑ What is your purpose of going to the United States?

Why do you go to America?

答：To participate in the Summer Work and Travel Program.（一定要回答参加赴美带薪实习项目）

☑ Why do you participant this program?

Why did you want to take part in this program?

答：Because I like American culture, I want to broaden my horizon, improve my English and make more friends. （参加项目是为了提高口语，增长阅历，结交更多的朋友等。围绕着文化交流我自提高去回答。）

☑ Who decided/made the decision for you to visit America?

答：Myself, and my parents also thought it was a good idea. （我想去，我父母也支持我。表明家人的意愿）

## 关于机构、美国基金会

☑ Who is your program Sponsor?

答：My Sponsor is ……（要回答DS2019表显示的全名，不要回答缩写）

☑ What does your Sponsor provide for you?

答：They will help me during my stay in America, such as ……（提供帮助和协助你们在美国顺利完成项目）

☑ Who is your Agency in China?

答：Western Exchange.（西米的英文）

## 项目具体内容

☑ What is the Summer Work and Travel program?

Please tell me the definition of this program.

What is your understanding of this program?

What is the purpose of attending this program?

答：Summer Work and Travel Program provide foreign students with an opportunity to live and work in the United States during their summer vacation to experience American culture and to be exposed to the people and the way of life in America. （这个项目主要是给在校大学生提供一个了解美国文化，提高英文水平，在美国工作旅游的机会）

☑ How did you know this program?

Where did you learn about this program?

答：I learned it from my school.

Through the promotion of my university.

I knew this from the Internet.

My friend joined this program before and recommended to me.

（可以回答从学校知道的，或者从网上知道的，同学朋友介绍得知的）

☑ How much you pay for this program?

答：I paid 18300RMB.

☑ When will you begin your work?

When will your program start?

答：I will start my work on June 15th.（参照DS-2019表项目开始的时间）

☑ When will you finish your work?

答：I will finish the work on September 15th.（参照DS-2019表项目结束的时间）

☑ How long will you work/stay in America?

答：about 3 months.（根据自己DS2019表上的项目时间回答）

☑ When will you plan to come back to China?

答：I will be back around ……（这个日期大致是DS2019表项目结束时间后的一个月内）

☑ What is your plan after you finish your work?

答：I will travel to XX city.

I will travel around to ……

（比如哪个城市看球赛、看演唱会、参观博物馆等等，但是千万不能说拜访同学亲戚）

## 第二方面

## 申请人与中国的纽带关系或约束力

根据美国移民法，申请人必须与居住国有牢固的约束力，促使其在访问美国之后按期回国。这些约束力包括：在中国的家庭（父母）、在中国的舒适生活、在中国未完成的学业等。涉及这方面的调查，签证官的问题会包括：

## 学生身份、学校

☑ Where are you studying? Where is it?

What is your major? What are you studying at school?

Where is your school?

What university do you study?

What grade are you in?

Which year are you in?

What do you study?

What's the field of your study?

Are you a student or do you work in the company?

答：I am a …（大一freshman、大二sophomore、大三junior、大四senior）student of XXXX University. My major is ….（在回答这个问题的时候，给签证官递上你的学生证、学校彩页介绍）

☑ When will you graduate?

How long will you be graduated?

Do you still have some years to graduate?

答：There has XX years left.

☑ What the main courses are you learning? Which one do you like best?

答：There are XX（多少）courses.（举例说明一下有什么课程）I like …best, because of…（简单谈一下为什么喜欢）

☑ How much is your tuition fee?

答：My tuition fee is …（要知道自己学费怎么说）

☑ How many students in your university?

答：There are XXX（多少） students in my university.

☑ How many colleges and what majors your school owns?

答：There are XXX（多少）colleges / majors in my university. Such as ……（举例说明一下学院和专业）

☑ What is the gender distribution?

What is the male to female ratio?

How many girls and boys in your school?

答：It is around XXXX（多少）girls and XXXX（多少）boys.

## 学校暑期放假时间

☑ How long is your summer vacation?

答：My summer vacation is from …to…（回答这个问题的时候一定要把你的放假证明和在校证明拿出来递给签证官）

☑ Do all the students in your university have the same summer vacation?

答：I am not sure about other students. However, my summer vacation is from…to… （回答这个问题的时候一定要把你的放假证明和在校证明拿出来递给签证官）

## 家庭情况

Do you have any siblings?

Do you have any brothers or sisters?

## 第三方面

## 其他问题

除了你的基本信息和项目信息，签证官也会问一些其他很随机的问题，从这些问题中判定你是否有移民倾向：

## 个人爱好

☑ Do you have any hobbies?

答：Sports: Basketball, football, table tennis, badminton, swimming, skating etc. (Which player do you like best?)

Music: Singing songs, pop music, classic music, playing the piano, singers, music star etc.

Movies: Action movies, comedy, tragedy, cartoon, local film, foreign film etc.

Reading: Novels, fiction, prose, poet, etc.

（简单说一下喜欢看哪个队的哪个球员，哪个歌手的哪首歌就可以了。如果不知道不要侃侃而谈，以免你听不懂。）

## 出国相关问题

☑ Have you been to other countries before?

Have you ever been abroad?

答：Yes I have been to ……for XXXX（多长时间）\ No. （如果出过国，简单说一下是哪些国家）

☑ Have you ever been to America?

答：Yes \ No. （如果去过美国，简单说一下之前去美国干什么去来了）

☑ Who are you traveling with?

Will you go to America alone?

答：I am alone. But if I can find some other J1 students who work in the same place, I will go with them.

I will go with some other J1 students.

（如果是一个人出行,在出行之前我能联系到相同企业的同学，那么我们会尽量一起出行）

☑ If you come across the difficulties in America, how do you handle/solve it?

答：If happened during my work I would ask my employer for help. If happened in my housing I would ask my host for help. In the event of an emergency, I would call 911. （如果是工作岗位遇到问题，寻找雇主；如果是住房方面的问题，寻找房东；同时会请美国 sponsor 协助解决，注意，一定是协助你去解决！如果遇到更加紧急的状况，会拨打 911）
