# 面试与口语准备 (Interview Prep)

## 核心原则

Sponsor 面试和雇主面试的核心不是考雅思托福，而是确认：
1. **你能听懂指令** (Can you follow instructions?)
2. **你敢于开口交流** (Are you willing to speak?)
3. **你性格开朗** (Are you outgoing?)

## 自我介绍模板 (Self Introduction)

> 技巧：微笑，自信，语速适中。

Hi, my name is [Name], and I am a senior student currently studying [Major] at [University].

My hometown is [City], a beautiful coastal city in [Province]. It's famous for [Food/Scenery].

I am an outgoing person (MBTI is ENFJ!). In my spare time, I enjoy sports and photography. For example, I used to be a lifeguard/runner... [Add personal story].

The reason I want to join the SWT program is that I want to experience American culture deeply and improve my English skills. I'm really looking forward to working with you!

## 常见问题 (Q&A)

### 1. 学校与专业 (School & Major)
*Q: What do you study? Why?*
A: I'm majoring in Software Engineering. I chose it because I like building things that people can use. It gives me a sense of accomplishment.

### 2. 为什么参加项目 (Why SWT?)
*Q: Why do you want to come to the USA?*
A: I want to step out of my comfort zone, see the real America beyond movies, and make friends from all over the world.

### 3. 工作相关 (Job Related)
*Q: How do you handle conflict with a colleague?*
A: I would stay calm and communicate first. I believe most conflicts are just misunderstandings. If we talk respectfully, we can solve it.

*Q: What if you get fired?*
A: I will follow the rules strictly so I don't think that will happen. But if it does, I will contact my Sponsor immediately for help.
