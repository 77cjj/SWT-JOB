On arrival: Shut off 1st-floor porch lights · 2nd-floor porch lights · flip switch to the right of the front door when you walk in · then clock in.

Before Breakfast / Prep

  1. Preheat ovens · take bakes out of the fridge
  2. Bake / warm items as per today’s menu
  3. Bring fruit platters & bakes up to porch 15 minutes before breakfast starts
  4. Start coffee · put out both regular & decaf Coffee available 30 min before breakfast
  5. Put water, juices & creamers into mini fridge
  6. Put flower vases on tables
  7. Turn on chafing dishes Water in pans · no hotter than 71°C / 160°F
  8. Warming trays on low heat Do not burn food
  9. Put out all table items: serving utensils & plates
  10. Put out buffet signs for specials GF · Dairy Free · Vegan · etc.
Keep everything clean & restocked. Communicate with guests.

During Breakfast

  1. Always wear gloves Cleanliness & hygiene are very important
  2. No personal phone use during service Music OK · keep phone on for front desk / owners
  3. Serve guests · restock hot items when low
  4. Keep coffee thermoses full enough
  5. Keep water, juices & creamers full enough
  6. Pastry & fruit platters mostly full, clean, presentable If dirty / not presentable → swap for a fresh plate
  7. During breaks: restock · wipe buffet & dining tables
  8. Check garbage — do not let it overflow
  9. Remove dirty food trays from trash by entrance → wash
  10. Keep serving utensils clean Switch out or wash between uses
  11. Restock utensils, napkins, plates, etc.

Service Quick Scan (every ~15 min)

  • Coffee / decaf levels
  • Hot food pans
  • Fruit & pastry look
  • Drinks & creamers
  • Utensils / napkins / plates
  • Trash & dirty trays

J–K · Prep for next day

  • Make fruit platters for next day
  • Fill cereals & oatmeal bags

2 · Clean

Use wet cloth with soap AND sanitizing spray on all tables & surfaces.
  • Buffet & dining tables
  • Coffee / tea thermoses
  • Fireplace station (utensil area)
  • Garbage cans / lids · entrance garbage cover
  • Chafing dishes / warming plates (microfiber)
  • Electric griddle (non-abrasive sponge)
  • Switch out dirty pastry / fruit plates & platters
  • Consolidate quiches & leftovers in aluminum containers
  • Consolidate coffee into one thermos if low · wash empty
  • Change water in flower vases · put centerpieces on tray (tables fully clear)
  • Dirty towels → laundry · bring clean towels up for next day
  • Sweep & mop floors

3 · Other / Close Out

  • Take inventory of all items (use Page 1)
  • Lock main refrigerator
  • Check equipment: ice machine, tea thermos, fridges, etc.
  • Chafing dishes / warming trays OFF
  • Leftovers → basement kitchen · left fridge
  • Garbage bags INSIDE trash cans by basement door White bags go inside black bags (squirrels / bugs)
  • Clock out · check in with front desk Communication is key
