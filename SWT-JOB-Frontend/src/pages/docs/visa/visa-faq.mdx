# 签证常见问题

一句话摘要：J-1 面签高频问答，按「赴美目的」与「国内约束力」两大维度组织。

## 面签核心逻辑

签证官默认申请人有移民倾向（214(b)），需在约 2 分钟内证明：

1. **赴美目的合理合法**——参加 Summer Work and Travel 文化交流项目
2. **国内约束力足够**——学业、家庭、回国计划明确

回答方式：自信、微笑、目光交流，往往比答案本身更重要。

## 赴美目的类

### Q: What is your purpose of going to the United States?

**A:** To participate in the Summer Work and Travel Program.

### Q: Why do you want to take part in this program?

**A:** I want to experience American culture, improve my English, and make friends from different backgrounds.

### Q: Who is your program Sponsor?

**A:** 回答 DS-2019 表上的 **全称**（不要缩写）。

### Q: What is the Summer Work and Travel program?

**A:** It provides foreign students an opportunity to live and work in the United States during summer vacation to experience American culture.

### Q: How did you know this program?

**A:** From my university / the Internet / a friend who joined before.

## 国内约束力类

### Q: What will you do after the program?

**A:** Return to China to continue my studies / graduate and find a job in my field.

### Q: What is your major? Why did you choose it?

**A:** 如实回答专业名称和选择原因，与在校证明一致。

### Q: Do you have relatives in the US?

**A:** 如实回答。有亲属需说明关系，强调仍会按时回国完成学业。

### Q: Who will pay for your trip?

**A:** My parents / myself with family support.（与资金证明材料一致）

### Q: What is your plan after graduation?

**A:** 与回国计划一致，如继续深造或在国内就业。

## 工作相关

### Q: What kind of job will you do?

**A:** 根据 Job Offer 回答具体岗位（如 front desk attendant, server 等）。

### Q: Where will you work?

**A:** 回答 Job Offer 中的雇主名称和城市/州。

### Q: How much will you earn?

**A:** 根据 Offer 中的时薪回答，不要夸大。

## 注意事项

- 所有回答与 DS-160、Job Offer、在校证明 **信息一致**。
- 不要背诵过长答案，自然表达即可。
- 口语测试 7 分以下的同学建议提前做模拟问答。
- 详见 [面签流程](/docs/visa/visa-interview) 和 [面试准备](/docs/apply/interview)。
